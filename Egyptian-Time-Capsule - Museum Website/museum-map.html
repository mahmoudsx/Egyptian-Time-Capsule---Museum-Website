VISITOR GUIDE

Tips for Your Visit

Make the most of your museum experience with these helpful tips

Best Times to Visit

Early mornings (9-11 AM) and late afternoons (3-5 PM) typically have fewer visitors. Weekdays are generally less crowded than weekends.

Suggested Routes

Start from the ground floor and work your way up. Each floor is organized chronologically, making it easier to follow the historical narrative.

Photography Guidelines

Photography is allowed in most areas without flash. Look for designated photo spots for the best angles and lighting.

Family Visits

Pick up our family activity pack at the information desk. Interactive exhibits are marked with special symbols on the map.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about navigating the museum

How long does it take to explore the entire museum?

A complete tour typically takes 2-3 hours. However, we recommend allocating 3-4 hours if you want to explore all exhibits thoroughly and participate in interactive experiences.

Are guided tours available?

Yes, we offer guided tours daily at 10 AM, 1 PM, and 3 PM. Special themed tours are available on weekends. Book at the information desk or in advance through our website.

Where can I store my belongings?

Lockers are available on the ground floor near the main entrance. Small lockers are free of charge, while larger ones require a small fee. All lockers are secure and monitored.

Is there a recommended route for quick visits?

For visits under 90 minutes, follow our "Highlights Route" marked in gold on the map. This route covers the most significant exhibits across all floors.
