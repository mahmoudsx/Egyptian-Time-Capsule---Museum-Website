FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions about visiting the museum

Can I bring food and drinks into the museum?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted inside the museum. The Nile Café offers a variety of refreshments and meals.

Is there a coat check or luggage storage?

Free coat check and lockers are available near the main entrance. Large bags must be stored during your visit.

Are guided tours included in the admission?

General admission includes a self-guided audio tour. Guided tours are available for an additional fee and can be booked online.

What happens if I lose something during my visit?

Lost items are kept at the Information Desk. Contact our lost and found service at +20 123 456 7890.
